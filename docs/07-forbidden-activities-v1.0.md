# ProofMoney Forbidden Activities v1.0

**Version:** v1.0  
**Project:** ProofMoney  
**Initial Author:** Vingen Motoki  
**Positioning:** An open protocol for verifiable money integrity  
**Core Statement:** If money cannot be verified, it is only a promise.

---

## 1. Purpose

This document defines activities that are not permitted, not endorsed, and not recognized by ProofMoney.

ProofMoney is an open protocol initiative focused on verifiable money integrity.

It is not a fundraising campaign.  
It is not an investment product.  
It is not a yield program.  
It is not a trading community.  
It is not a private allocation scheme.

---

## 2. No PRM Sale

ProofMoney does not sell PRM.

Forbidden activities include:

- selling PRM before public protocol release;
- offering PRM through private sale;
- offering PRM through public sale;
- offering PRM through pre-sale;
- offering PRM through insider sale;
- offering PRM through discounted allocation;
- offering PRM through whitelist purchase;
- claiming to sell future PRM rights.

Any person or entity claiming to sell official PRM before public rules exist should be treated as unauthorized.

---

## 3. No Private Allocation

ProofMoney does not recognize private PRM allocation.

Forbidden activities include founder allocation, insider allocation, investor allocation, advisor allocation, influencer allocation, market maker allocation, exchange allocation, hidden address allocation, or undocumented release agreements.

---

## 4. No Yield or Return Promise

ProofMoney does not promise profit.

Forbidden activities include:

- promising guaranteed returns;
- promising fixed yield;
- promising price appreciation;
- promising future income;
- promising exchange listing gains;
- promising liquidity;
- promising buybacks;
- claiming early participation guarantees profit.

PRM may never have market value.

---

## 5. No Investment Promotion

ProofMoney official channels must not be used for investment promotion.

Forbidden activities include:

- price predictions;
- trading calls;
- buy signals;
- sell signals;
- leverage recommendations;
- “early wealth” narratives;
- urgency-based purchase pressure;
- guaranteed upside claims.

---

## 6. No Test Unit Speculation

ProofMoney test environments are for testing only.

Test units have no monetary value.

Forbidden activities include buying test units, selling test units, creating test unit markets, claiming test units will convert into PRM, or advertising testnet participation as an earning opportunity.

---

## 7. No Fake Airdrop Claims

ProofMoney does not recognize unauthorized airdrop claims.

Users should treat all airdrop claims as suspicious unless confirmed through official ProofMoney documentation.

---

## 8. No Impersonation

No person may falsely claim to represent ProofMoney.

Forbidden impersonation includes fake official accounts, fake support accounts, fake founder accounts, fake contributor accounts, fake wallet download links, fake PRM sale websites, fake grant programs, and fake public fund proposals.

---

## 9. No Private Key Collection

ProofMoney will never ask users for private keys or recovery phrases.

Users should never share private keys or recovery phrases with anyone.

---

## 10. No Market Manipulation

ProofMoney does not support market manipulation.

Forbidden activities include coordinated pump campaigns, artificial volume creation, wash trading, false liquidity claims, fake exchange listing rumors, and using official channels to influence short-term price.

---

## 11. No Unsafe Software Distribution

No one should distribute unsafe or misleading ProofMoney-related software.

Forbidden activities include fake wallets, modified wallets that steal keys, malware disguised as ProofMoney tools, phishing websites, fake node clients, fake Proof Explorer mirrors, and unauthorized downloads using ProofMoney branding.

---

## 12. Summary

ProofMoney forbids activities that create hidden privilege, false financial expectations, or user harm.

ProofMoney is a verification protocol.

Its purpose is not to create hype.

Its purpose is to make monetary claims verifiable.

If money cannot be verified, it is only a promise.

**No privilege. No false promises. No hidden claims.**
